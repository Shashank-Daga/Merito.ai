Established in 1953, Shyam Steel Group is one of the leading TMT Bar Manufacturers and Suppliers in India, manufacturing TMT Rebar, Billets, and Sponge Iron. With an annual turnover of Rs 3000 Crores, Shyam Steel is associated with the largest institutional projects in India.<br />
Guided by a philosophy to produce safe and sustainable steel, it is a pioneer in quality production of steel.

### Mission-critical priority
Driven by the need to expand its business portfolio, Shyam Steel is on a journey to create an omnichannel e-commerce platform catering to the construction and architecture industry. With this journey comes the need to build a team from scratch for which they need candidates with the right skill set who would help them scale exponentially.

### How Merito helped
With Merito’s consultative approach, Shyam Steel was able to close most of its positions in a record timeframe. Merito brought light to their vision and venture by creating awareness and helping them to target the right candidates for the positions.<br/>
Through Merito’s screening and assessment, the average time to hire a candidate was reduced ensuring a fast and accurate way of hiring. By identifying the candidates who fit their culture, Shyam Steel can engage in efficient employee retention. 

### Merito’s Impact
Working with Merito, Shyam Steel has been able to build a tech team by hiring for roles including but not limited to:

- **Program Manager**
- **Project Manager**
- **Business Analyst**
- **UI/UX Developer**
- **MEAN Full Stack Developer**

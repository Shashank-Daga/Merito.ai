Mosaic Wellness is a digital-first, multi-brand consumer goods company owned and operated under one house, with each of its brands working together toward one mission: To help Indians lead more fulfilling life surrounded by wellness and grace.
With the help of cutting-edge technology & customer-first products, Mosaic has been simplifying health solutions for its customers.

### Mission – critical priority
Mosaic has been growing exponentially and with this growth, it is looking to hire key roles in its marketing and customer success teams that match the right candidate for the job. Despite the urgency of the hire, Mosaic was always very critical of the talent they hire, and often candidate goes through multiple rounds of interviews before he gets hired.

### How Merito Helped
Merito deployed role-based assessments to identify matching candidates to the job roles. The personality assessments also helped to get an in-depth perspective before their interview.
Merito created awareness about the company among prospective candidates that helped Mosaic to identify RIGHT candidates through assessments and closed all the positions within the stipulated timelines.

### Merito’s Impact
Working with Merito, Mosaic Wellness has been able to accelerate its growth by hiring for roles including but not limited to:
- **Head Customer Success**
- **Lead Amazon Marketing**
- **Graphic Designer**
- **Motion Designer**

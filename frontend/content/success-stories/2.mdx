Mindshare, Wavemaker, and Essence Mediacom — are the agencies of GroupM that are on the front line to a new era of Media Strategy And Innovation. According to COMvergence, GroupM has two of the top five global media agencies and more than $60 billion in annual media spend, providing access and scale everywhere their clients do business.

Unique in services, culture, and ideas, each brand is a leader in their field. Through a range of leading businesses in advanced TV, AI-driven programmatic, eCommerce, and more, GroupM has leveraged collective intelligence to make advertising work better in the world.

### Mission – Critical Priority
With a growing business of all its media agencies, GroupM was looking to hire roles across its mid to leadership levels to help achieve efficiency in the organization.

### How Merito Helped
Merito helped GroupM to identify matching candidates through its targeted sourcing approach. Our screening has helped GroupM to expedite its hiring process.
We helped GroupM to hire key leadership roles across strategy, data, and analytics functions. The quicker hiring in its media functions helped GroupM to close key roles in time bound manner. The offer-to-joiner ratio also improved to 70%.

### Merito’s Impact
Working with Merito, GroupM has been able to reduce the hiring time and improve its offer-to-joiner ratio by hiring for roles including but not limited to:

**Leadership Roles**

- **AVP Strategy**
- **Director Analytics**
- **Tech Architect**
- **Data Solutions**
- **Client Engagement**

**Middle and Senior Management Roles**

- **Media Planner**
- **SEM/PPC**
- **Paid Social**
- **Media Analytics**
- **Programmatic Media**

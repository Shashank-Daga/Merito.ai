# Building a High-Performance Sales Team from Scratch

## The Sales Challenge
A fast-growing consumer goods company needed to build a sales team from the ground up to capitalize on market opportunities. They lacked experienced sales leadership and struggled with inconsistent performance.

## Strategic Team Building
Merito designed a comprehensive sales organization development program:

### Sales Leadership
- **Sales Directors**: Experienced leaders with track records in scaling sales teams
- **Regional Managers**: Territory experts with local market knowledge
- **Sales Trainers**: Specialists in sales methodology and coaching

### Field Sales Force
- **Key Account Managers**: Relationship builders for major retail partners
- **Territory Sales Executives**: Local market penetration specialists
- **Channel Partners**: Experts in distributor and wholesaler management

### Support Functions
- **Sales Operations**: Process optimization and CRM implementation
- **Business Intelligence**: Sales analytics and forecasting experts
- **Marketing Coordinators**: Lead generation and campaign support

## Performance Achievements
- **300% revenue growth** in 18 months through expanded market reach
- **50 new distributor partnerships** established nationwide
- **85% improvement** in sales team productivity
- **40% reduction** in customer acquisition costs

## Success Methodology
1. **Competency Framework**: Defined clear skills and behaviors for success
2. **Structured Training**: Comprehensive onboarding and continuous development
3. **Performance Management**: Data-driven coaching and incentive systems
4. **Technology Enablement**: CRM and sales automation tools

The company's sales transformation not only achieved remarkable growth but also established a scalable sales model that continues to drive sustainable business expansion.

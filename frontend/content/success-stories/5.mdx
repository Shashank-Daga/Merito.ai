The Baker’s Dozen (TBD) is India’s largest hand-crafted Artisan bakery brand, known for the authenticity of products and processes, with 40+ stores and more than 1000 touchpoints online & offline in the marketplace.
Most of their products are made with all-natural ingredients, without preservatives, and are 100% wholewheat.

### Mission – critical priority
With Baker’s Dozen going through a big transformation and massive scale-up post their seed funding, the client wants to hire key roles in the business and support functions to accelerate its growth and elevate the customer experience.

### How Merito Helped
Through Merito’s targeted sourcing, Baker’s Dozen was able to identify the matching candidates effectively and efficiently.
Merito helped in closing all the positions within the average time to hire of 3-4 weeks including key positions of Vice President Finance which took a 2 weeks’ time.

### Merito’s Impact
Working with Merito, Baker’s Dozen has been able to accelerate its growth by hiring for roles including but not limited to:
- **VP Finance**
- **Head of Marketspace**
- **Head D2C**
- **Manager – Performance Marketing**
- **Data Analysts**
- **HR Operations**
